Overwatch is a multimedia franchise centered on a series of online multiplayer FPS video games developed by Blizzard Entertainment.

Overwatch released in 2016, and Overwatch 2 released in 2022.

Both games feature hero-based combat between two teams of players vying over various objectives, along with other traditional gameplay modes.
